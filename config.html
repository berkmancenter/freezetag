URL:
The TagTeam URL for the JSONP resource which you want to display
Items to Load:
FreezeTag loads only a certain amount of items and loops through those
Color Scheme ID:
FreezeTag provides 8 default color schemes to help style multifeed pages. Based on The Solarized Theme
Time per Animation (seconds):
How long a news item takes to appear
Time Between Animations (seconds):
After a news item appears, how long until the next one should appear
Times to Iterate Through Feed Before Updating:
The amount of times FreezeTag will show each news item before fetching updated news
Max amount of items on the page at once:
After how many items should FreezeTag remove the item from the page
Tags: True False
Whether tags will be displayed or not
Hide Overflow: True False
Whether FreezeTag simply hides items before they would leave the visible area
